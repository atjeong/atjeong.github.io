Google Scholar | LinkedIn


I am a Ph.D candidate at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST) advised by Professor Jaegul Choo.

My research focuses on trustworthy AI, with a particular emphasis on debiasing, fairness, and safety. I have explored debiasing tasks in classification models and recently expanded my interests to address societal bias and safety in models trained with large-scale datasets, especially diffusion-based text-to-image models and multi-modal LLMs.
